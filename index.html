Petros Ghazaryan’s Most Frequent Guests

For this article, we analyzed 429 of those interviews, which were conducted with 46 guests who each appeared on the show at least five times.

These frequent guests were divided into two categories: members of Armenia's ruling party - Civil Contract, and other guests. This categorization allowed us to further explore the dynamics and representation of Civil Contract's political figures.



Despite receiving numerous invitations, Edmon Marukyan's appearances on the state channel, for example, have decreased over time. A breakdown by year reveals that, he appeared less frequently on Petros Ghazaryan's show, and by 2024-2025, he was no longer a guest.

What About Time Allocation in Petros Ghazaryan’s Interviews

Based on our analysis of 429 interviews, we calculated the total amount of time spent on interviews with members of Armenia's ruling party, Civil Contract, and compared it to other guests. Our data shows that 52.7% of the total interview time was dedicated to ruling party representatives. Is this proportion significant? The decision is yours to make.

The 'Other' category includes individuals from the opposition, neutral experts, and those with a pro-ruling party reputation. You can refer to the table above for a detailed breakdown of these guests, which will help provide a clearer understanding of who they are.
